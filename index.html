Who I Am ↓

I began my career in marketing, but quickly shifted toward the technical foundation behind campaigns - learning to code (yes, I custom coded this site), analyzing data, and managing the tools that keep operations running. As I took ownership of key platforms like Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Asana, Clarity, Bynder, and Relay Network, I found myself driving process improvements, leading cross-functional initiatives, and coordinating technical work across teams.

That progression evolved into a Tech/PM style role where I bridge strategy, systems, and execution. I’m motivated by making complex workflows simpler, smarter, and more effective through a mix of marketing insight and technical expertise.
